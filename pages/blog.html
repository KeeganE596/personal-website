13 May 2019

Cast-Iron Plant Model

I started a new plant model, cause who needs to finish other projects first...Based on the cast-iron plant, a popular indoor variety, I thought it looked pretty cool and not to diffucult to model. I used 3dCoat to get a flat leaf shape, then moved to Maya and used nonlinear deform tools, band and twist to shape the leaves. I alse made a quick spherical planter to stick the leaves in. Hopefully i'll get to texturing the small scene and finishing it off.

10 May 2019

Monstera Leaf Model

It's been a while but here is an update on the Monstera leaf model/textures. I made a quick little scene to put the leaf in with a pot and attached a stalk, on a grey infinty wall background. I set up a couple lights and used an HDR lightdome for better ambient lights. I UVd the leaf in Maya and chucked the model over to Substance Painter to start the texturing process. Following along with Brent Le Blancs tutorial I finished a first attempt, I am not overly happy with it, it looks pretty fake and I don't have any sub-surface scattering happening yet, but it is a start and the result isn't terrible.
